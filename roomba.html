Dilir the Roomba

Roomba background information here:

The name of the Roomba is Dilir. This name came to be because Adam and Ethan were in math class. One of their assignments included a process called D.I.L.I.R the robot and it just kinda clicked with us.

Roomba Controls

Credit to Ethan Prospere, Eli Coleman and Adam Lahkiky

P - Passive

S - Safe

F - Full

C - Clean

D - Dock

R - Reset

Space - Beep

Arrows - Motion

Other Important Information

If the Roomba ever stops moving while attempting to control it press the P and then the S key
